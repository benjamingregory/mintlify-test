## What is Coplay?

Coplay is a powerful AI assistant designed specifically for Unity game developers. It integrates directly into your Unity Editor, providing intelligent assistance to enhance your game development workflow and productivity.

With Coplay, you can:

- Get real-time coding assistance and suggestions
- Solve development challenges with AI-powered guidance
- Streamline your Unity workflow with contextual awareness of your project
- Collaborate more effectively within your game development team
