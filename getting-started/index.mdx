Coplay is a powerful AI assistant designed specifically for Unity game developers.
It integrates directly into your Unity Editor, providing intelligent
assistance to enhance your game development workflow and productivity.

With Coplay, you can:

- Streamline your Unity workflow with contextual awareness of your project
- Rapidly explore and prototype and explore new experiences
- Automatically create multiple versions of assets and scenes 
- Get real-time coding assistance and suggestions
